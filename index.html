Welcome

My name is Kevin Li (李文亮), a second-year PhD student at Gatsby Computational Neurosicence Unit of University of London. I am supervised by Maneesh Sahani and Peter Dayan. I did my undergraduate and M.Eng at University of Cambridge with final project supervised by Máté Lengyel. I also took the Cambridge-MIT Exchange program in 2012-2013.

From 2014-2015 I took a gap year during which I interned at Microsoft Research Cambridge with Sebastian Nowozin on road network identification from satellite images and at Tsinghua University with Prof Funchun Sun and Dr Chunfang Liu on robotic grasp planning.
